Stages of Bangladesh Education System

The education system in Bangladesh is formulated according to the National Education Policy and is divided into three main streams: primary, secondary, and higher education. Alongside general education, there are parallel systems of madrasa education and technical education. The use of Bengali language as a medium of instruction is emphasized.

Pre-Primary Education

Age: 4-5 years

Details

Age: 4-5 years

Main Objective: Preparing children for school

Availability: Available in government and private primary schools

At this stage, children are taught through play, songs, drawing, and basic social skills to prepare them for primary education.

Primary Education

Grade: 1-5

Details

Grade: 1st - 5th

Age: 6-10 years

Special Feature: Compulsory and free education

Institutions: Government primary schools, Ibtedai Madrasas

The government distributes free textbooks at this level and runs scholarship programs for students.

Junior Secondary Education

Grade: 6-8

Details

Grade: 6th - 8th

Streams: Available in general and madrasa streams

Assessment: Previously JSC/JDC, now continuous assessment

At this stage, students learn basic subjects like science, mathematics, Bengali, English, social sciences, and religious studies.

Secondary Education

Grade: 9-10

Details

Grade: 9th - 10th

Groups:

  • Science
  • Humanities
  • Business Studies

Examinations:

  • SSC (General Education)
  • Dakhil (Madrasa)
  • SSC Vocational (Technical)

Higher Secondary Education

Grade: 11-12

Details

Grade: 11th - 12th

Examinations:

  • HSC (General)
  • Alim (Madrasa)
  • HSC Vocational (Technical)

At this level, students choose groups according to their career goals and study in colleges.

Technical & Vocational Education

Skill-based Education

Programs

  • SSC (Vocational)
  • HSC (Vocational)
  • Diploma in Engineering (4 years)
  • Diploma in Nursing / Medical Technology

Institutions: Polytechnic Institutes, Technical Schools and Colleges

The Bangladesh government has undertaken extensive programs to promote technical education to create skilled human resources.

Higher Education

University Level Education

a. Bachelor / Undergraduate

Duration: 3-4 years

Degrees: BA, BSc, BCom, BBA, BS, BSS, BEd

Provided by: Universities and Colleges

b. Master / Postgraduate

Duration: 1-2 years

Degrees: MA, MSc, MBA, MBS, MSS, MEd

Higher education is provided in public and private universities in Bangladesh.

Professional Education

Career-focused Education

Professional Degrees

  • MBBS / BDS (Medical)
  • Engineering (BSc in Engineering)
  • LLB / LLM (Law)
  • CA (Chartered Accountant)
  • CS, CIMA, ACCA

These are specialized degrees for high-paying professions and have their own professional bodies.

Teacher Education

Teacher Training

Training Programs

  • DPEd (Primary Teacher)
  • BEd (Bachelor of Education)
  • MEd (Master of Education)

There are Teachers Training Colleges and institutions under the education department for professional development and training of teachers.

Research & Highest Level

Advanced Studies

a. MPhil (Master of Philosophy)

Research-based degree which usually takes 1-2 years to complete.

b. PhD (Doctor of Philosophy)

The highest level of education in Bangladesh, Doctor of Philosophy. Usually takes 3-5 years for research and thesis submission.

At this level, students mainly contribute to creating new knowledge and scientific research.
